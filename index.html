ILS Doctoral Research Forum 2025


Welcome to the Department of Information & Library Science Doctoral Research Forum 2025 (ILS-DRF 2025)


The ILS Doctoral Research Forum (DRF) is a yearly event that focuses on motivating Ph.D. students to participate in innovative research through the lens of information and library science, social sciences, policy studies, technolgies and other interdisciplinary fields.

This year ILS-DRF 2025 will be held on Nov. 1st, 2025 and we encourage all PhD student to submit their finished or ongoing research ideas at the forum. The forum provides a great opportunity for doctoral students to present and discuss their research with peers and faculty members.

Presentation time will vary depending on the number of presentations. We expect a maximum of 10 minutes to present, followed by 5 minutes Q&A.

As in the past, there will be monetary prizes for the presentations ranked 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place by the ILS professors! So make sure to submit a presentation. :)

Please contact us if you have any questions.


Important Dates

  • Oct. 3rd, 2025: First call for papers.
  • Oct. 10th, 2025: Second call for papers.
  • Oct. 17th, 2025: Submissions are due.
  • Oct. 19th, 2025: Presenters receive notifications and presentation guidelines.
  • Oct. 30th, 2025: PowerPoint presentation slides are due.
  • Nov. 1st, 2025: DRF presentations.
